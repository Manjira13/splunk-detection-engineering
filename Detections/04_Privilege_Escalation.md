
# Privilege Escalation

## Overview

Privilege Escalation techniques allow adversaries to obtain higher-level permissions than originally granted. Attackers commonly target local administrator privileges or privileged domain groups to expand control over the environment.

Detecting privilege escalation is essential for identifying attackers before they gain unrestricted access to critical systems or sensitive data.

This document contains the planned privilege escalation detections maintained within this repository.
